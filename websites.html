Our Web Design Process

A proven 4-step approach to creating healthcare websites that convert

01

Discovery

We start by understanding your clinic's unique needs, target patients, and competitive landscape. Through in-depth consultations, we identify your goals, brand voice, and the specific features your practice requires. This phase typically takes 1-2 weeks and ensures we build a foundation for success.

02

Design

Our designers create wireframes and mockups tailored to the healthcare industry. We focus on building trust through professional aesthetics while ensuring intuitive navigation for patients of all ages. You'll review and approve every design element before we move to development.

03

Development

Using modern, lightweight code, we build your site with healthcare-specific features like online booking integrations, HIPAA-compliant forms, and mobile-responsive design. Our development process prioritizes speed, security, and search engine optimization from day one.

04

Launch

After rigorous testing across all devices and browsers, we deploy your site with proper analytics setup. We provide training on content management and offer ongoing support. Most websites go live within 4-6 weeks from project start, getting you online faster than traditional agencies.

Website Design FAQ

Common questions about our healthcare website services

How long does it take to build a healthcare website?

Most GP clinic websites are completed within 4-6 weeks from initial consultation to launch. This includes discovery, design, development, and testing phases. Rush options are available for practices with urgent needs.

Do you integrate with practice management systems?

PMS integration is currently in our development pipeline. We're working to enable seamless integration with popular Australian practice management systems including Best Practice, Medical Director, and HotDoc for online bookings, patient forms, and appointment management.

Is the website mobile-friendly?

Absolutely. Every website we build is fully responsive and optimised for mobile devices. With over 70% of patients browsing on mobile, we ensure your site looks great and functions perfectly on all screen sizes.

What's included in your website packages?

Our packages include custom design, up to 10 pages, contact forms, Google Maps integration, basic SEO setup, mobile responsiveness, and 3 months of support. Premium packages add features like booking integrations, patient portals, and advanced analytics.

Can I update the website content myself?

Yes, we build sites with user-friendly content management systems. We provide training so you can update text, images, and blog posts without technical knowledge. For major changes, our support team is always available.

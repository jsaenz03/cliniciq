Meet the Founder

Meet the Founder

John Saenz

Registered Nurse | Information Systems Graduate | Founder, ClinicIQ Solutions

Hello! I'm John Saenz, the founder and driving force behind ClinicIQ Solutions. As a Registered Nurse with over 5 years of hands-on experience in GP clinics, I've lived the daily challenges that healthcare professionals face - from mountains of paperwork to inefficient workflows that take time away from patient care.

My journey into healthcare technology began with a simple realization: the gap between clinical care and administrative efficiency was costing practices precious time and resources. With a Bachelor of Information Systems Management & Accounting from Victoria University and membership in the Golden Key Society, I bring a unique dual perspective - understanding both the clinical realities of healthcare delivery and the technical solutions that can transform practice operations.

Throughout my nursing career, I've handled troubleshooting systems, optimising workflows, and identifying gaps between clinical teams and technology. This hands-on experience, combined with my background in Quality Assurance and Data Analytics, gave me the insights needed to build solutions that actually work in real-world healthcare settings.

ClinicIQ Solutions isn't just another tech company - it's a collaborative partnership driven by a genuine passion for helping healthcare practices thrive. We partner with experienced nurses, IT professionals, and NDIS organisations to deliver comprehensive solutions and support for NDIS-relevant concerns. When you work with ClinicIQ, you're working with a team who understands your challenges because we've faced them ourselves.

Our Mission

First of all, I give glory to God the Almighty - The Father, Jesus Christ the Son, and The Holy Spirit.

ClinicIQ Solutions emerged from a vision to help nurses, GP clinics, and small businesses thrive through intelligent automation and digital transformation.

My mission is simple: to give healthcare professionals back the time they need to focus on what matters most - patient care. By automating repetitive tasks, streamlining workflows, and providing intelligent tools, I help practices reduce administrative burden while improving efficiency and compliance.

Every solution I create is built with a deep understanding of healthcare workflows, ensuring that technology serves the needs of clinicians rather than creating additional complexity.
